* The (m)otherboard Manifesto v1.0 *

(m)otherboard believes:

Mothering takes many forms. By (re)turning to our origins, we bend the arc of technology from individual convenience to collective good.

*

We are interdependent—across generations, chosen kin, communities of care, and mother earth.

*

We listen to the wisdom of our children, elders, and ancestors.

*

Stories are technology. Songs are technology. Rituals are technology. Houses are technology. Tables are technology.

*

Technology is not neutral.

*

Progress is nonlinear. The spiral cycles between past, present, and future.

*

The future is not inevitable. It emerges from our collective imaginations.

*

Feelings, instincts, yearnings, and dreams are meaningful signals.

*

Machine learning can never replace embodied intelligence.

*

Rupture is inevitable and repair is divine.

*

Governance is a tool for moving forward, together.

*

Clarity is care. Everyone benefits when values are made explicit.

*

We thrive in community, both online and on the ground.

*

The margins are where true innovation emerges.

*

Relying on the generosity of billionaires is a bad plan.

*

We are planting seeds for what comes after the fall.

*

We cheat death not by becoming immortal, but by building a world where future generations thrive. Legacy, not life extension.

=

We are nurturing technology you’d be proud to bring home to your mother.
