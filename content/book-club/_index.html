About the Book

We Just Build Hammers brings to life the stories of the unsung heroes of responsible tech and the science fiction that inspired them. The book spans 100 years of history: from the advent of the atomic age, to the formative years of computing; from the open source revolution of the 2000s, to the tech justice movement of today.

This book challenges technologists to consider for themselves whether they’re really just “building hammers”—neutral tools whose potential for good balances their potential for harm—or if they are unwittingly contributing to systems that exacerbate inequality, inequity, and injustice.
