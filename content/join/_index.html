Join the community as a (m)otherboard supporting member.

Supporting members build lasting connections in a community of their peers, coming together to help bend the arc of technology toward collective good.

How to join

  1. First, sign up for our email transmissions:

  2. Next, pledge your support as a sustaining member. As part of our commitment to economic justice, we offer a sliding scale for membership. Make a monthly donation of any amount, or support us for a year with a one-time donation.

  3. Watch for a welcome email providing details about your membership and perks.

Membership benefits

As a supporting member, you get all the privileges of membership in the community, including having a say in how (m)otherboard operates, and the projects we take on, through our participatory governance model.

Other membership benefits include:

  • A ticket to our exclusive virtual book club event

  • Access to future members-only events including panel discussions with leading figures in the responsible tech movement

  • A free sticker pack by mail

  • An invitation to the (m)otherboard community slack (coming soon)
