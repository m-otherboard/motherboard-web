Join the community as a (m)otherboard supporting member.

Supporting members build lasting connections in a community of their peers, who are doing the hard work of bending the arc of technology toward better techno-social futures.

How to join

  • Sign up for our email transmissions:

  • Make a monthly donation of any amount, or support us for a year with a one-time donation.

  • Watch for an email from us welcoming you to the community and providing details about your membership.

(Contact us if you would like to join but need financial accommodations.)

Membership benefits

As a supporting member, you get all the privileges of membership in the community, including having a say in how (m)otherboard operates and the kinds of projects we take on through full participation in our transparent governance model.

Other membership benefits include:

  • An invitation to our virtual book club event series and a discount code for 20% off our current book club selection.

  • Access to future members-only events including panel discussions with leading figures in the responsible tech movement.

  • An invitation to the (m)otherboard community slack (coming soon).
