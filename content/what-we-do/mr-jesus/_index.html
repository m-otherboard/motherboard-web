* Mr Jesus *

Mr Jesus follows Chris, a down-on-his-luck actor who wakes up one morning to discover that his image has been stolen to make a GenAI Jesus, and now everyone believes he's the Second Coming.

Impact Campaign

Motherboard is partnering with Mr Jesus to screen the film with faith, community, and tech organizations to spark conversations about the tech stories reshaping our world.

A fundraising event cemented the approach: an improv comedy night in a church basement proved that laughter opens the door to honest talk about AI-- our fears about its impact on relationships, work, community, and the planet.
