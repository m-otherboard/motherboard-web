* Hire Us *

As society grapples with the unchecked power of the tech oligarchy and the speed of technological change, children's candid perspectives and creative ideas are essential to questioning our assumptions and finding new paths forward

All Kids Considered is a (m)otherboard produced podcast that amplifies young voices on key issues related to technology. While adults debate tech's impact on youth, we rarely hear directly from children themselves. Our podcast will give kids of all ages-- from toddlers to teens-- a platform to share their personal stories, hopes, and visions about technology's role in all of our lives.

Content

Each 15-30 minute episode will feature young people from a diverse range of backgrounds exploring a common theme, with an adult host conducting interviews and weaving stories together.

Early episodes may include:

  • The mobile phone
  • AI and creativity
  • Social media
  • Choosing to unplug: neo-Luddite flip-phone teens
  • Youth Digital Wellbeing Movement
  • Tech and AI in the classroom
  • More topics crowdsourced from young people

Audience

Adults and older youth interested in and concerned about the present and future of technology.
