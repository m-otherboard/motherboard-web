A People's History of Tech

APHOT emerged from The Tech We Want program as a collective storytelling project designed to make visible the often-hidden human and environmental impacts of technology. Through successful installations at major events and a growing digital archive , APHOT has demonstrated the power of participatory history-making in challenging traditional tech narratives.

Visit the People's History of Tech website.

Responsive Tech Present

RTP builds responsive, participatory and nimble systems to counter present day tech disruption. This includes tech-enabled participatory governance, grant-making, and Guaranteed Basic Income to sustain tech justice workers and nurture radical dreaming.

Intergenerational Tech Futures

ITF is our R & D studio where we explore the future of tech we wish to see across generations. This includes intergenerational, place-based convenings and media projects that lift up young people's perspectives on the future of tech that they wish to see.

Book Club

The (m)otherboard book club gives members of our community the opportunity to learn about and discuss the vital perspectives that fuel the responsible tech movement. Our book club meetups feature readings by the author, questions and answers, and open group discussions. Learn more about our current selection.
