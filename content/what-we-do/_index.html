A People's History of Tech

A People's History of Tech (APHOT) reframes dominant technological narratives by documenting how diverse communities have experienced, shaped, and resisted technological change. This project moves beyond established inventor-centric histories to capture contested, hidden, and forgotten pasts by amplifying marginalized voices through participatory storytelling and archival methods.

Visit the People's History of Tech website.

(m)otherboard Book Club

The book club gives members of our community the opportunity to learn about and discuss the vital perspectives that fuel the responsible tech movement. Our book club meetups feature readings by the author, questions and answers, and open group discussions. Learn more about our current selection.

Responsive Tech Present

We're building responsive, participatory and nimble systems to counter present day tech disruption. This includes tech-enabled participatory governance, grant-making, and Guaranteed Basic Income to sustain tech justice workers and nurture radical dreaming.

Intergenerational Tech Futures

Our R & D studio explores the tech futures we wish to see across generations. This includes intergenerational, place-based convenings, and media projects that lift up young people's perspectives on the future of tech.
