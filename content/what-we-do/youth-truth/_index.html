* Youth Tell the Truth about Tech *

As society grapples with the unchecked power of the tech oligarchy and the speed of technological change, listening to children's experiences, candid perspectives, and creative ideas is essential to questioning our assumptions and finding new paths forward.

Its' time to listen to kids

Youth Tell the Truth about Tech is a storytelling project that amplifies young voices on key issues related to technology. While adults debate tech's impact on youth, we rarely hear directly from them. In contrast, our community-based activations, interactive installations,and podcast give kids of all ages-- from toddlers to teens-- a platform to share their personal stories and future visions about technology's role in all of our lives.

How We're Doing It

  • Pop-up gatherings across the country where young people create art and share their tech personal stories and future visions, in partnership with community institutions such as schools, libraries, and local festivals.

  • Interactive installations to showcase and generate art and personal stories from young people.

  • Podcast and short videos highlighting youth perspectives on topics such as:

    • AI and creativity
    • Social media
    • Choosing to unplug: neo-Luddite flip-phone teens
    • Youth Digital Wellbeing Movement
    • Tech and AI in the classroom
    • Alternative tech futures
